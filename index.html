About Me

My name is Fady Naguib. I was born and raised in Cairo, Egypt. After finishing highschool, I decided to move to Arizona, where I had some family living, in order to pursue a better future and a more luxurious career in the field of chemical enginerring. After graduating from Arizona Sate University with a BSE of chemcial engineering in 2017, I began looking at the different idustries and fields that interested me. As my research went on, I realized that the market for software developers and software engineers was very hot. Now, during my engineering undergraduate days, I often was very amazed by sotware such as MatLAB, which made complex engineering calculations rather simple and less time consuming. I began liking the idea of developing solutions to complex problems using software, which led me to the coding bootcamp in University of Arizona. I'm very glad I discovered my passion early on in life and am able to pursue an immersive program that can teach me all the necessary skills and tools in order to be successful in the path I chose to take. In this portfolio, I plan on documenting my path towards learning web development and turning my passion into a successful career.
